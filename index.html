About

Dave Marsh Glass and Glazing LTD has been trading since October 2015. I have over 30 years of experience in the glass and glazing trade, now specialising in smaller door and window installations, repairs and maintenance work. I undertake work for Dorset County Council and am CRB checked. I am happy to work across the Dorset area. I can provide:

Services Provided:

  • 24 hours call out and boarding up service
  • Installation of double glazed windows and doors
  • Replacement of double glazed units in to UPVC, wood, and aluminium frames (including Velux windows)
  • Repairs to patio doors (replacement of rollers and locks)
  • Handle, hinge, and window mechanism replacements on all types of windows
  • Lock replacement on UPVC and aluminium doors
  • Overhead concealed door closures supplied and fitted (Dorma)
  • Cat and dog flaps installed into glass or panels
  • Sash cord replacements
  • Panic bars repaired and installed
  • Privacy/solar control film supplied and fitted
  • High level window openers (Teleflex) supplied and installed
