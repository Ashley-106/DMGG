Domestic

We cover a wide range of Domestic services. We provide these services across the entire Dorset area.

Domestic Glazing

Glazing into existing aluminium/PVCu/wood doors/windows. Single glazing to doors/windows, i.e face putty. Single glazing greenhouse glass replacement into aluminium/wood.

Window Furniture

Hinges and handles for aluminium/PVCu/wood. Including fire-stay hinges and restricted hinges. Sash cord mechanisms repaired and fitted into wood/PVCu vertical windows.

Door Furniture

Handles, hinges, locks and mechanisms for aluminium/PVCu/Composite/wood doors. Patio door rollers.

Cat flap and letter boxes

Fitment and installation or Cat Flaps into PVCu or glass. Letter boxes fitted into aluminium/PVCu/wood.
